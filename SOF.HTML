SOF Schools

The Science Olympiad Foundation (SOF) is a New Delhi-based, non-profit educational organization established in 1998 to promote science, mathematics, computer education, English, and general knowledge among school students (grades 1–12) in India and internationally. SOF conducts annual, prestigious Olympiad exams—including NSO, IMO, IEO, and NCO—to foster academic competition and nurture future talent.
